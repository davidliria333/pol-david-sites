The challenge

Your firm is losing hours to work that does not require a law degree

High-stakes legal work gets squeezed when teams are underwater on repeatable tasks. These are the patterns we see most often.

Manual document review consumes associate time on pagination, duplication checks, and first-pass summaries—before anyone reads for substance.

Case research and precedent search stretch across databases and folders, slowing motions and memos when speed matters.

Client updates, intake follow-ups, and status emails repeat the same patterns—yet they still pull lawyers and staff off billable work.

Contract and clause review cycles run for days because redlines, playbooks, and comparisons are done by hand.

Private AI built for legal workflows
