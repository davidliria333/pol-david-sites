Implementation Guide for Shopify Stores

Successfully implementing automated Shopify support requires systematic planning and phased rollout to ensure seamless customer experience during transition.

Phase 1: Assessment and Planning (Week 1)

  • Ticket Analysis: Review 3-6 months of support history to identify patterns
  • Query Classification: Categorize queries by type, complexity, and frequency
  • Integration Planning: Map existing tools (Shopify, email, chat platforms)
  • Success Metrics: Define KPIs for response time, resolution rate, satisfaction

Phase 2: AI Training and Setup (Week 2-3)

📚 Knowledge Base Creation

  • Product catalog integration
  • Policy documentation upload
  • FAQ database creation
  • Historical resolution examples

🔧 System Integration

  • Shopify API connection
  • Order management system sync
  • Email platform integration
  • Chat widget installation

Phase 3: Testing and Optimization (Week 4)

  • Shadow Mode: AI processes queries but human agents review all responses
  • A/B Testing: Compare AI vs. human response quality and speed
  • Edge Case Collection: Identify scenarios requiring human intervention
  • Response Refinement: Adjust AI responses based on customer feedback

Phase 4: Gradual Rollout (Week 5-8)

🚀 Rollout Strategy

Week 5: 25% of simple queries (order status, tracking)
Week 6: 50% of routine queries (returns, exchanges)
Week 7: 75% of standard queries (product info, policies)
Week 8: 90% full automation with human escalation
