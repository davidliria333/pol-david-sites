Frequently asked questions

How quickly can I get started?

Most coaches are up and running within 2-3 weeks. This includes the methodology capture phase, platform training, and testing to ensure everything works perfectly with your coaching style.

What if clients prefer working with me directly?

The platform complements your high-touch coaching, not replaces it. Many coaches use it for their broader audience while maintaining premium 1-on-1 services for top-tier clients.

How does the revenue sharing work?

You keep the majority of revenue. We charge €39/month per active client plus the one-time setup fee. You set your own pricing and keep everything above our fee.

Can I customize the platform?

Absolutely. The Professional and Enterprise plans include custom branding, personalized responses, and integration of your specific training methodologies and nutritional philosophies.
